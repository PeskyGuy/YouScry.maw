Free District Street Bard - Mana Harp Performance

23,891 views | Added: 2 weeks ago | By: VyrodinStreets
★★★★★ (4.8 / 5.0)

Description:

Incredible performance by a street musician in Free District's main market. The mana-harp is a traditional instrument that uses the player's personal mana to create ethereal sounds. This bard has been performing in Free Market for over 10 years and is a local legend.

The song is "Echoes of Victory," a popular folk tune commemorating the end of the Civil Centennial War. Notice how the mana flows create visible light patterns in sync with the music - that's advanced technique!

Location: Free District, Vyrodin
Recorded: 28 Summer 2005 A.U.
Equipment: Standard Communication Scroll with audio capture
