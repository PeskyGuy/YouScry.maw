Description:

Official performance of the Union Anthem at the Victory Day ceremony, held at the Central Spire on 15 Spring 2005 A.U.

The Union Anthem celebrates the founding of The Union and the end of the Civil Centennial War. This performance features the Vyrodin Symphony Orchestra and the Church Choir, conducted by Master Harmonist Vex.

Victory Day commemorates the defeat of Tyrant Vaxara and the establishment of peace 70 years ago.
