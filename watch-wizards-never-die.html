Wizards Never Die

234,567 views | Added: 3 weeks ago | By: ArcaneProductions
★★★★★ (5.0 / 5.0)

Description:

A powerful testament to the enduring spirit of magic and those who wield it. "Wizards Never Die" explores the immortal legacy of great mages throughout history, from the Heroes of the Union to the legendary artificers of today.

This scrying has become a cultural phenomenon across Ohsa, inspiring a new generation of magic users and reminding us all that the spirit of innovation and wonder lives on through every spell cast and every enchantment woven.

The title references the famous saying attributed to Archmage Minoph: "A wizard's work never ends, for magic itself is eternal. In that sense, wizards never truly die - they become part of the mana that flows through all things."
